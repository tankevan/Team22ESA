Operational Concept

Concept Sketch

Key Features

  1. User must be able to move character around by clicking around the stage to perform various actions
  2. User must place trash into their respective bins to "score points"
  3. The Global Health Bar indicates the progress of the user. The user wins when the Global Health Bar is completely green
  4. The user will have a choice to spend time convincing other characters above to join him in the clean up
