User Personas

From the three group headings, we categorised the consumers into three distinct personas:

  1. Enthusiastic

    She is very concerned about saving the environment and plays her part in daily conservation efforts. Our game does not target her.

  2. Powerless

    He is aware of the need for conservation but feels that his individual effort to save the environment is not going to make an impact.

  3. Disinterested

    He has no interest in saving the environment. Even if he does, he feels that it is not worth his time to make changes to his daily routine.

Quality Attributes

From this survey, we inferred some necessary quality attributes the game must have to fulfil our mission of encouraging environmentally friendly choices:

  1. The game shall empower its users to make an impact on the environment
  2. The game shall instill in its users the idea that every individual's effort counts to making an environmentally friendly community
  3. Since the target audience is generally adverse towards conservation, the game shall be able to send across the message in a short time frame
  4. The game shall be designed to engage first and foremost, only then will it be able to educate
